About the Author

Hi! I'm Khaliya Ross, a junior undergraduate majoring in Information Technology at Kennesaw State University. I currently work in IT, where I apply real-world skills in requirements management, change control, and web development. I enjoy painting, gaming, cooking, and exploring new tech trends.

This project, The Changing Face of the Web, is part of my IT3203 coursework. It explores the evolution of websites from static HTML pages to immersive, intelligent platforms. My goal is to present technical insights in a clear, engaging way while highlighting the ethical and creative dimensions of modern web development.
