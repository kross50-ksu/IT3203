The Changing Face of the Web

A technical study of website development from its origins to its future.

Welcome

This site offers a comprehensive journey through the evolution of the web, beginning with the earliest static HTML pages that laid the foundation for online communication. It examines the milestones that transformed websites into dynamic, interactive environments, highlighting the rise of CSS for design, JavaScript for interactivity, and frameworks that enabled single‑page applications and progressive web apps. Along the way, it explores key design principles such as responsive layouts and accessibility standards that ensure inclusivity. Finally, it looks ahead to the future of digital experiences, where websites are becoming immersive, intelligent platforms shaped by emerging technologies and innovation.
